Global Water Partnership

India is an active member of the Global Water Partnership, an international network that promotes integrated water resources management (IWRM). Through this platform, India collaborates with other countries to share knowledge, strategies, and technologies related to water conservation and management.
Global Water Partnership India is a leading network dedicated to fostering sustainable water management and enhancing water security across the nation. As part of the larger Global Water Partnership network, it brings together government bodies, civil society, private sector, and academic institutions to address critical water challenges facing India today. The organization works towards innovative, integrated, and participatory approaches to water management, emphasizing the importance of water conservation, equitable distribution, and climate resilience. By promoting best practices, policy advocacy, and capacity building, Global Water Partnership India aims to ensure that every community has access to safe and sufficient water resources. Through collaboration and strategic partnerships, it strives to create a future where water is not only preserved for current generations but also sustainably managed for those to come, ensuring a resilient and prosperous India.
